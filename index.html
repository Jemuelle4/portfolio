Empathy

Empathy is an important part of being an actor. Through empathy, one can (or at least attempt to) understand other's perspective. Being a part of multiple productions, I have developed my empathy skills to better understand other people and their perspectives. I can apply this skill in UX/UI design to gain user perspective and conciously design products that will work for all users.

Collaboration

My involvement in productions, students organizations, and committees requires me to work with a diverse group of people. Whether it be planning events for our community members or working on plays, collaboration is key for a successful end product. With my experiences, I can effectively collaborate with different departments and stakeholders throughout the design process to arrive at a better product.

Creativity

In the rehearsal room, I am constantly looking for new and different ways to express lines through the use of tones and physicallity until I find one that best tells the overall story. Applying this skill of brainstorming creative ideas to design will help arrive to new and innovative solutions.

Adaptability

The first production I acted on was an improvised show. In improv, I am constantly adapting to the situation and its spontaneous nature. In addition, improv requires creativity and collaboration with others to make the scene come to life. I can use my skills of adaptability in design to respond accordingly to new and different circumstances.
