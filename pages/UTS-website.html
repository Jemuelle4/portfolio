Undergraduate Theater Society Website

Why redesign the website?

When I decided to redesign the website, my initial reasoning was to simply move our website to a better website builder. After taking a class about design method in spring 2020, I decided to apply the skills and processes I learned to make the user experience better.

Undergraduate Theater Society is, first and foremost, an organization that provides theater opportunity for undergraduates. Ranging from acting to designing to theater administration to stage managing, and more.

The old website provided information about our seasons and shows: what the show is about, when it is showing, and where to buy tickets. It was showcase-oriented. It lacked information about how undergraduates can get involved and what opportunities are available for students: our core purpose.

This redesign aims to address that issue and make it easier for undergraduates to find information about the various opportunities we offer.
